This site is lovingly developed in Python, HTML and a wee bit of Javascript, working directly with Tumblr's API, and served up on the Flask microframework. It relies heavily on open source software and will soon be released on Github.

This site is meant as a simulation of the original installation at the Indianapolis Museum of Contemporary Art as part of the Global Spaces exhibition, curated by Ben Valentine.
